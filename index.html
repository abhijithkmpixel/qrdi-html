Strategic Focus

QRDI 2030 presents a bold and ambitious vision that delivers a holistic change in the RDI ecosystem through seven transformation elements. QRDI 2030 outlines five national priority areas into which future RDI activities will be directed to help address national challenges and make the most of Qatar’s RDI opportunity.

Strategic Initiatives

The Qatar Research, Development, and Innovation (QRDI) Council has several strategic initiatives in place to achieve this goal, including promoting partnerships between academia and industry, investing in emerging technologies, and supporting the development of skilled talent in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields. These initiatives are aimed at driving economic growth and diversification in Qatar and positioning the country as a global leader in innovation and scientific research.
